TITLE: Financial Services and Reimbursement Guidelines

CATEGORY: FINANCE
PRIORITY: MEDIUM

CONTENT:
This document outlines financial processes for customers and employees.

For Common Users (Customers):
Payments and Transactions:
Customers can make payments using supported payment methods such as cards, UPI, or net banking. If a transaction fails but the amount is deducted, it is usually auto-refunded within 5–7 business days.

Refund Policy:
Refunds are processed as per company policy and depend on the service or product purchased. Customers can track refund status through their account dashboard.

Billing Issues:
If customers notice incorrect charges or duplicate payments, they should raise a support ticket with transaction details.

Invoices and Receipts:
Customers can download invoices and payment receipts from their account section.

For Employees:
Salary and Payslips:
Salaries are credited on a monthly basis. Employees can download payslips from the payroll portal. Any discrepancies should be reported to the finance team.

Expense Reimbursement:
Employees may claim reimbursements for approved expenses such as travel, meals during business trips, and work-related purchases. All claims must be supported with valid bills and submitted within the specified timeline.

Tax Declarations:
Employees are required to submit tax declarations and proofs annually through the payroll system. Failure to do so may result in higher tax deductions.

Bonus and Incentives:
Bonuses and performance incentives are awarded based on company performance and individual evaluations, subject to management approval.

Provident Fund and Insurance:
The company provides statutory benefits such as provident fund contributions and health insurance coverage as per government regulations.
