TITLE: HR Policies and User Assistance Guidelines

CATEGORY: HR
PRIORITY: LOW

CONTENT:
This document describes HR-related policies and general user assistance topics.

For Common Users (Customers):
Customer Support Hours:
Customer support services are available during official business hours. Queries raised outside these hours will be addressed on the next working day.

Escalation Requests:
If a customer is not satisfied with a response, they may request escalation. Escalated tickets are reviewed by senior support staff.

Profile Information Updates:
Customers can update personal information such as contact number and email through their profile settings. For issues, support assistance can be requested.

For Employees:
Leave Policy:
Employees are entitled to different types of leaves including casual leave, sick leave, and earned leave. Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.

Attendance Policy:
Employees are expected to log in during official working hours as defined by the company. Any deviation or shift changes must be approved by the manager in advance.

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may request work-from-home arrangements subject to manager approval and company guidelines. Prolonged remote work requires HR approval.

Employee Onboarding:
New employees will receive onboarding emails with details regarding system access, company policies, and training schedules. Any missing access should be reported to HR.

Exit Process:
Employees planning to resign must serve the notice period as mentioned in their offer letter. Exit formalities include knowledge transfer, asset return, and clearance from IT and HR departments.
